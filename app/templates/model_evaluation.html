📐 Metric Definitions

Accuracy: Overall % of correct predictions.
Precision: Of predicted informal, how many truly are.
Recall: Of actual informal businesses, how many detected.
F1-Score: Harmonic mean of Precision and Recall.
AUC-ROC: Discrimination ability across all thresholds.

📐 Regression Metric Definitions

MAE: Mean Absolute Error — average error magnitude.
MSE: Mean Squared Error — penalizes large errors more.
RMSE: Root MSE — same units as the target variable.
R²: Coefficient of determination — % of variance explained (1.0 = perfect).

💡 Why Low R² Is Expected

The INFORMALITY_INDEX is a binary-derived composite (mean of binary flags). Its variance is highly concentrated at 0.0 and 1.0. Continuous regression on a near-binary target yields low R² by design — classification is the appropriate approach for this variable.
