Project Goal

Apply K-Means clustering to the EMICRON dataset to automatically segment Colombian micro-businesses by informality profile, giving policymakers actionable city-level intelligence.

🗄️

Dataset · EMICRON 2023

Official DANE survey of micro-businesses with up to 9 employees. 81,018 records across 24 departments + Bogotá D.C., covering 7 modules including formalization variables (RUT, ARL, Cámara de Comercio).
