Variable Analysis — Key Formalization Indicators

Distribution of the most important variables for informality detection (from EMICRON Module F):

🪪
P1633 · Has RUT (tax ID) Binary: 1=Yes, 2=No. Key formal registration indicator. Nationally ~77.6% of micro-businesses have NO RUT (2024).
🏛️
P1055 · Chamber of Commerce Binary: 1=Yes, 2=No. In Bogotá (2023) only 27.1% of micro-businesses were registered.
🏥
P3091 · ARL Contribution Occupational risk insurance contribution. 93.6% of owners made no ARL contributions in 2024 (EMICRON).
📋
P640 · Accounting Records 5-category: balance sheet / daily log / informal notes / financial reports / no records. 46.2% kept no records (Bogotá, SDDE-ODEB 2023).
💰
P3088 · Monthly Sales Previous month sales in COP. Continuous numeric variable. ~7% imputation rate due to non-response.
🔢
P3053 · CIIU Sector Code Economic activity classification (CIIU Rev. 4 AC). Covers agriculture, manufacturing, commerce, construction, transport, services.
