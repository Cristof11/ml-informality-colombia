🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

EDA is the process of summarizing, visualizing, and understanding the main characteristics of a dataset before applying any model. For this project, EDA serves three purposes: (1) understand the distribution of informality-related variables across Colombian micro-businesses, (2) identify data quality issues — particularly missing values in key formalization indicators, and (3) detect correlations between features that will inform the K-Means clustering model. The EMICRON 2023 dataset was selected because it is the most comprehensive official source for micro-business informality in Colombia, with 81,018 anonymized records across 7 structured survey modules.

Data Quality Evaluation

✅ Strengths

Official DANE survey with strict quality control protocols. Uses CAPI (Computer-Assisted Personal Interviewing) via DMC devices. Data validated by a technical committee including DNP, MinCIT, and academia before publication. Anonymization protocol documented and publicly available.

⚠️ Known Issues

Self-reported income (P3088) has ~7% missing rate due to non-response. Some categorical variables use numeric codes requiring mapping from the DDI/XML dictionary. 8 low-density departments excluded from sampling frame. Informality flags are binary and may undercount partial compliance cases.

Variable Analysis — Key Informality Indicators

Distribution and interpretation of the most important variables for informality detection (EMICRON Module F):

🪪
P1633 · Has RUT (tax ID) Binary: 1=Yes, 2=No. Key formal registration indicator. Nationally ~77.6% of micro-businesses have NO RUT (2024). Strong predictor of informality cluster.
🏛️
P1055 · Chamber of Commerce Binary: 1=Yes, 2=No. In Bogotá (2023) only 27.1% of micro-businesses were registered. Strong signal of formal business identity.
🏥
P3091 · ARL Contribution Occupational risk insurance. 93.6% of owners made no ARL contributions in 2024. Strongest single binary predictor of informality.
📋
P640 · Accounting Records 5-category: balance sheet / daily log / informal notes / financial reports / no records. 46.2% kept no records (Bogotá, SDDE-ODEB 2023).
💰
P3088 · Monthly Sales Previous month sales in COP. Continuous numeric variable. Log-normally distributed. ~7% imputation rate due to non-response bias.
🔢
P3053 · CIIU Sector Code Economic activity (CIIU Rev. 4 AC). Covers agriculture, manufacturing, commerce, construction, transport, services. Excluded: utilities, finance, air transport.
👥
P3034 · Number of Workers Total workers including owner. Range 1–9 (EMICRON definition). Larger workforce slightly correlates with higher formalization probability.
📅
P3035 · Months in Operation Business age in months. Older businesses show marginally higher RUT registration rates. Useful for temporal stratification of clusters.
